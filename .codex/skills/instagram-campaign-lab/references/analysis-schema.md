# Instagram Reference Analysis Schema

Use this rubric when the user supplies screenshots, handles, captions, notes, metrics, or examples from Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, Pinterest, or other social accounts.

## Account DNA

- Account: handle/link.
- Platform: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, X, Pinterest, or other.
- Category: competitor, inspiration, adjacent market, aspirational.
- Audience: who the account appears to serve.
- Promise: what the account seems to be known for.
- Visual tone: premium, raw, editorial, playful, minimalist, dense, nightlife, wellness, etc.
- Content mix: reels, TikToks, Shorts, carousels, feed photos, stories, lives, testimonials, offers.
- Repeatable formats: named series, before/after, walkthrough, POV, product demo, recap, FAQ, comment reply, stitch/duet-style response, trend adaptation.
- Campaign behavior: promos, launches, seasonal pushes, giveaways, event drops, urgency.
- CTA pattern: DM, booking, link, comment, save, share, call, visit.
- Metrics to capture: views/plays, retention/watch time if visible, likes, comments, shares, saves, profile visits, link clicks, follower/subscriber changes.
- Proof: reviews, numbers, transformations, clients, press, venue/product evidence.
- Risks: copying risk, copyright, claims, privacy, regulated promises, platform concerns.

## Pattern Translation

For every useful pattern, convert it:

- Reference pattern: what works.
- Why it works: audience/psychology/format reason.
- Our version: original implementation for the user's brand.
- Platform-native version: how it should look on TikTok vs Instagram vs Shorts or another selected network.
- Assets needed: photos, videos, copy, proof, offer, template.
- Approval needed: publish, ad spend, third-party asset, testimonial, music, account outreach.

## Final Deliverables

- Brand-safe creative rules.
- 10-20 photo ideas.
- 5-10 reels/TikTok/Shorts scripts or hooks.
- 2-4 campaign concepts.
- 1 monthly posting rhythm per selected platform.
- Prompts for imagegen/Canva/fotografo.
- Originality check.
